Intro — Hola! I am research fellow @ the AI Group in the Universitat Pompeu Fabra (UPF) in Barcelona. Since Fall 2024, I am fellow of the "LaCaixa" Foundation via one Junior Leader grant. This basically means that both my position and research activities are fully funded for the next 3 years. Additionally, I am member of the ELLIS society.

Research — In the past, I spent quite a lot of time working on Gaussian processes, probabilistic AI/ML methods and Bayesian inference. In the present, my curiosity is focused on the connections between modern machine learning algorithms (i.e. self-supervised learning), architectures (i.e. transformers) and well-known frameworks like Bayesian modelling, information theory and the rigorous principles of generalization. Often, I also work on continual learning and model merging in several projects with collaborators.
